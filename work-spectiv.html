Spectiv

Most finance apps shout numbers at you. Spectiv was designed to do the opposite — quiet, confident, and easy to trust.

The idea

Investing apps tend to feel like trading floors — red, green, and relentless. For this concept we imagined the opposite: a calm, premium product that helps everyday people build wealth without anxiety. The brand leads with reassurance; the interface leads with clarity.

Brand

A deep-ink navy grounds the brand in trust, with a soft mint for growth and a lavender accent for a modern, human edge. Editorial type keeps it premium rather than techy.
